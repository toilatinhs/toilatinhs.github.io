FASHION GONE ROGUA

Cold Laundry

Cold Laundry Escape the noise. COLD LAUNDRY is a clothing brand founded in 2019 by Ola and Cerise Alabi. We believe in “escaping the noise”. The premise behind this is to escape the roughness, grit, and monotonous routine of everyday life. It is a place of peace and an aesthetic experience. Our clothes are meant to inspire, calm, and bring about happiness and pleasant thoughts. This philosophy is reflected through the tones, designs, and the locations we capture our imagery. As the main photographer behind the brand, I feel fortunate to have an opportunity to work with a brand that not only shares my creative vision in photography, but allows me to execute with such freedom and control. If you would like to see more of my work for the brand, you can do so here, COLD LAUNDRY.
